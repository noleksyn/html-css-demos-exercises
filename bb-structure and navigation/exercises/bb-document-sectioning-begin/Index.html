Leagues

We offer both classic and head to head leagues, as well as private and public leagues.

Classic Leagues:
In classic leagues teams are ranked based on the total number of points scored by their players throughout the season.
Head To Head Leagues:
In head to head leagues each team plays against another opponent each week of the season. Just like the Premier League, a win is worth 3 points, a draw is worth 1 point, and a loss is worth 0 points.
Private Leagues:
Private leagues work the same way as public leagues except they require a password to join them. Private leagues also allow the league creator to manually start the draft with the click of a button. Public leagues start automatically at a given date. Also, settings for private leagues can be changed before the draft starts, whereas the settings in public leagues can't be changed.

Picking a well balanced team.

This refers to both price and teams. Teams: Selecting your FPL team from a range of teams ensures you are well covered in the event that one side does particularly well or badly in any given weekend. Price: Ensure that the selection of 1-3 high price players is not at the expense of the rest of your team. It is important that not only 1-3 players are relied upon to score all of your points. If there is an injury, or if the players form drops, it can hurt your points in a big way.
